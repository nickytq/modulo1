Braille Project

Our project seeks to bring technology closer to people with visual disabilities through a physical keyboard based on the Braille system. With this proposal, we aim to promote inclusion, facilitate communication, and encourage equal opportunities in educational and technological environments.

Discover Project Back Home

What does our project consist of?

The project consists of developing a physical keyboard inspired by the Braille system. Its purpose is to allow people with visual disabilities to write and use technological devices in a simpler, intuitive, and independent way.

Objective

Develop an accessible tool that allows people with visual disabilities to interact with computers and other devices in a more comfortable and independent way.

Braille Keyboard

The keyboard uses six buttons that represent the six dots of the Braille cell. Each combination allows users to write letters, numbers, and symbols easily.

Inclusion

Our project seeks to eliminate technological barriers and promote equal opportunities through accessible solutions for everyone.

How does it work?

1

Press

The user presses a combination of the six buttons on the Braille keyboard.

2

Process

The microcontroller identifies the combination and converts it into a letter or symbol.

3

Send

The information is automatically sent to the connected computer or device.

4

Write

The character appears on the screen, allowing fast and accessible writing.
